FAQ

Have a question? It may be answered below!

Is it free?

Yes! The Open Leadership Library, much like a traditional library, doesn't cost anything to use. In fact, we will ship your books to you and provide a shipping label for their return. Absolutely no cost to you, even if you lose your return label.

Who started this?

The Open Leadership Library is hosted by the ML Learning Initiative at the MIT Media Lab. The books were originally donated by both the Learning Initiative and attendees of two Open Leadership Camps in 2017. Read more About Us.

Can I really write in it?

Yes! We encourage you to take notes, leave comments, highlight, annotate, draw... However you feel like interacting with the book. While you're at it, take photos of the book and send them to us. Bring it on vacation, on your commute, to your favorite cafe, etc. Just remember to return it to us.

How do I get a book?

To request a book, please use the Checkout form or click the link in the book's record on the Catalog page.

How long can I keep a book?

Your book is due 1 month after you receive it. This means, the book should be postmarked 31 days after your book delivery date.

I'm not done with my book! Can I renew?

Renewing a book is possible, as long as there is not a significant waiting list for the book. Send the librarian an email as soon as you know you'd like to renew.

What if I have a book to return?

Firstly, write a review of the book and leave it somewhere in the pages.
Then, send the librarian an email containing your name and mailing address. Within two business days the librarian will create a shipping label and email it to you. All you need to do is attach the mailing label and send the book back to us

Can I get a new shipping label?

Of course. Send the librarian an email with your name and address and a new label will be sent to you.

What if my book is late?

You will receive a sternly-worded overdue notice from the librarian via email. We recommend donating another book when you return the one you borrowed, but it is not required.

The book I want isn't available. Can I still request it?

You are more than welcome to request an unavailable book, but there are a few reasons why the book may be marked "unavailable" in the catalog. Reasons include:

  • Another patron may currently be borrowing the book
  • The book may have not been returned after the Open Leadership Camps
  • The book is about to be spotlighted on social media
  • The book is on its way back to the Library
  • A new copy of the book needs to be purchased

No matter what, when you request the book the librarian will send you an email to give you an idea of the book's status.
