Open Leadership  noun
\ ˈō-pən ˈlē-dər-ˌship \
The practice of mobilizing your community to help achieve the aims of your organization.

Library  noun
\ ˈlī-ˌbrer-ē \
a place in which literary, musical, artistic, or reference materials (such as books, manuscripts, recordings, or films) are kept for use but not for sale
